Why Choose Static Websites?

Static websites are fast, secure, and highly customizable. They are ideal for portfolios, blogs, and small business websites. Here's why you should consider static websites:

  • Speed: Static websites load faster as there is no server-side processing.
  • Security: Fewer vulnerabilities compared to dynamic websites.
  • Scalability: Easily scalable without complex backend systems.
  • Cost-Effective: Low maintenance costs with minimal hosting requirements.
